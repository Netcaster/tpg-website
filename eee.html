Platform Thesis

EEE is not a token platform.
It is an asset origination engine.

TPG-EEE sits between TPG's traditional treasury operations and digital custody infrastructure, enabling event, hospitality, promotion, and payment activity to be captured as structured value.

The model converts consumer engagement into tokenized value streams, aggregates those streams into treasury pools, and structures them as collateral supporting credit facilities, bonds, insurance instruments, and institutional capital.

Institutional Credit Thesis

The event is not underwritten as entertainment risk. It is underwritten as a contracted cash-flow and collateral origination environment with lockbox controls, borrowing base discipline, and DSRA reserves.

Token exposure is treated as supplemental, capped, and haircutted — not as the primary collateral basis.
